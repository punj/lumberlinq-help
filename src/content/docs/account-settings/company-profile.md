---
title: Company Profile & Branding
description: Manage your company's legal details, address, industry profile, and logo used across LumberLinq documents and exports.
---

Company Profile and Branding manage your business identity within LumberLinq. These settings affect how your company appears in exported PDFs, share links, and account communications. Open at `/company` and `/company/branding`.

## Identity Tab

The Identity tab contains legal and business information for your company.

![Company identity tab with Company ID](/screenshots/company/company-01-details-identity-company-id.png)

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Company ID | A unique identifier for your account. **Always include this when contacting support** — it lets the team locate your account instantly. |
| Company Name | The trading or legal name of your business |
| Tax ID / GST / VAT | Your business tax registration number |
| Phone numbers | Primary and secondary contact numbers (include country code) |
| Email | Company contact email |
| Website | Company website URL |
| Legal Entity Type | Private Limited, LLP, Partnership, Sole Proprietor, Foreign Entity, etc. |

Keep Identity details accurate — they appear on exported PDF documents and in account communications.

## Location Tab

The Location tab stores the registered address of your company.

![Company location tab](/screenshots/company/company-02-details-location-tab.png)

Select **Country** first — the State and City dropdowns load based on your country selection. Fill in Address Lines and Postal Code as needed.

## Profile Tab

The Profile tab stores supplementary business information.

![Company profile tab](/screenshots/company/company-03-details-profile-tab.png)

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Industry Type | Timber Trading, Lumber Manufacturing, Freight & Logistics, etc. |
| Company Size | Number of employees or revenue band |
| Role in Company | Owner, Director, Manager, etc. |

## Branding — Company Logo

Open `/company/branding` to manage the logo that appears on your exported PDFs and share links.

![Company branding logo page](/screenshots/company/company-04-branding-logo.png)

- **Logo URL field** — enter the URL of your hosted logo image (PNG or SVG; white or transparent background recommended)
- **Save** — applies the logo to all future exports
- **Use Default** — reverts to standard LumberLinq branding

**Logo not showing on exports?** The URL must be publicly accessible. Open it directly in a browser — if it prompts for a login or shows an error, the PDF generator cannot load it. Host the image on your website or a CDN.
